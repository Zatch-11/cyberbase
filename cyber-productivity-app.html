ABOUT THE APP
CyberBase is a free, all-in-one productivity app built specifically for cybersecurity students, ethical hackers, and anyone on a learning journey. No accounts, no subscriptions, no internet required after first load — everything saves locally on your device.

Whether you're grinding TryHackMe, preparing for eJPT, doing bug bounty, or just trying to stay consistent with your learning — CyberBase keeps you organized, focused, and on track.
